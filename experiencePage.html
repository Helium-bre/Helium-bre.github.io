    The MEC is an annual competition at McGill, made to challenge the most brilliant minds of the Engineering Faculty with complex designing problems.   • C:\> McGill Biomechanics \ Hardware Team

    Oct 2024 - Present

    The Biomechanics Team is focused on the design and implementation of an upper limb exoskeleton which is able to detect and counter Parkinson tremors in the patient's forearm. As a member of the hardware team, my goal is to implement electromyography (EMG) sensor and an inertial measurement unit (IMU) into the exo-suit. In the Fall 2024 semester, I realized the data extraction system for the two sensors, as well as part of the hardware design of our prototype.

  • C:\> McGill Vertical Flight Society \ Avionics Team

    Oct 2023 - Apr 2024

    The Vertical Flight Society (now MDVFS) is a team focused on the design and fabrication of an electric Vertical Take-Off Landing (eVTOL) aircraft. As a member of the avionics team, our goal was to specify all the electronic requirements of the system, and to find solutions to these requirements, by implementing sensors such as LIDAR, camera, GPS, and by making our own PCB. During the 2023-2024 year, I implemented the airspeed sensor on the eVTOL, and assisted on the design of different electronic components.
